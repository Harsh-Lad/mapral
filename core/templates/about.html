History of Mapral


Mapral Managerial & Allied Services (MMAS) has been incorporated on 09th March 2010 to provide integrated HR & Outsourcing Solutions to various business segments at Pan India level. MMAS is fast spreading its operations into various verticals of business segments and mainly concentrating its activities into IT & Non-IT sectors.

MMAS’s main objective was to provide complete management consultant most comprehensive services related to acquiring right talent pool, outsourcing of HR activities providing temp staffing solution, Personnel Management, contract staffing, statutory compliance, etc. MMAS believes in helping businesses by letting them focus on their core activities.

We introduce ourselves as a professionally managed Human Resource firm, rendering service for your entire payroll, statutory compliance, contract employees, recruitment and other HR needs.

We endeavour to reduce your workload, and be an integral part of your organization, so that you may have motivated people who will concentrate on your core activity and increase the business and profitability.
